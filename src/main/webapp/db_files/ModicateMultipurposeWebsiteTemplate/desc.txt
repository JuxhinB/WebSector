The template comes with 58 ready made unique pages, various headers, footers and content block allowing to create eye catching and truly flexible websites for any purpose and project. Clean and valid HTML5 code makes it easy to promote your website. Thought through layout is optimized for further theme conversion to popular CMS engines. Use Modicate to build professional websites that look and perform same great at desktop, mobile and tablet.
